Hidden Christmas Treasure

Find festive hidden objects in magical winter scenes

Puzzle iPhone & iPad Ages 4+

Discover magical Christmas settings and search for hidden items in a relaxed, festive puzzle. Sharpen your observation, enjoy warm décor and cheerful holiday atmosphere, and race the clock to find every object on the list—perfect for a quiet break or a family challenge.

What you will find

  • Multiple bright, Christmas-themed levels to explore
  • Classic hidden-object play—easy to start, satisfying to master
  • Charming art, cozy rooms, and seasonal cheer throughout
  • Smooth touch controls and a calm, laid-back pace
  • Fun for every age, whether you want to unwind or focus

How to play

  • Study each scene and read the list of items to find
  • Tap objects in the picture to collect them
  • Clear the list before time runs out to finish the level
  • Progress to unlock new scenes and tougher hunts
